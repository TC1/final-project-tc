Game 5 Eastern Conference SemiFinals 1997

The New York Knicks and the Miami Heat were in a grueling contest to claim top spot in the East. The series was a testament to both teams ability. The Knicks probably would have won too, had it not been for this fellow.

Here, seen on the cover of his latest Album. PJ Brown presents... PJ Brown,.Great Stocking stuffer.

Heat forward PJ Brown gave a hard foul to Knicks point guard Charlie Ward while scrambling after a missed foul shot.

Seen here, not in pain and agony.

Charlie Ward took boxing out seriously. He damn near took P.J. Brown's legs from under him which made P.J. angry beyond belief. Brown was so annoyed that he flipped Charlie with one arm like he was a little kid. Unfortunatley, for you, the NBA removed as many pictures of subsequent slam to the ground and benches clearing brawl off online as humanly possible.
