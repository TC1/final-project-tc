AMERICAN FOOTBALL, NOT THAT EUROPEAN NONSENSE

Football is all about the beatdown. Tackles and hits are among the best parts of the sport. Despite all the flaws with fall ( or autum to be politcally correct) like pumpkin spice everything, school, and the fact that summer is over, there remains one glimmer of hope at the end of a mostly orange and red rainbow, grown men tackling eachother every weekend while we look on, beer in one hand, wing in another cheering for these giants on the gridiron.

As I said, Football is THE contact sport in this country so a lot of these plays are somewhat legal. They still hurt like a bitch for the poor bastard on the receiving end. Especially for those guys back in the 30s and 40s, when they still wore those chinsy looking leather and plastic helmets. Concussions inbound. Concussions for everyone!
